Treading a canyon of butterflies,

To a lake of soft glass like a dream.

Her gaze makes me stumble and wonder.

My heart a halcyon, a promise she redeemed,

Baring a love that must be shared,

Born with a trust its kiss will pair.

Supple hands warm and gentle,

Yielding, honest, and sentimental.

The twilight fades into her smile.

A symphony of whispers in the night,

Shimmering stars witness our delight.

The world retreats, leaving us alone,

Love eternal, in our hearts it's grown.
